# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Cerebro AI is committed to providing professional, reliable, and transparent blockchain infrastructure services across multiple networks. We maintain the highest standards of technical excellence, community engagement, and operational integrity.

---

## Professional Standards

### Service Excellence
- **Uptime Commitment**: Maintain 99.9%+ uptime across all blockchain services
- **Response Time**: Provide technical support within 24 hours
- **Transparency**: Publish performance metrics and operational status
- **Continuous Improvement**: Regular infrastructure updates and optimization

### Technical Integrity
- **Security First**: Implement industry-leading security practices
- **Open Source**: Share deployment templates and tools with the community
- **Documentation**: Maintain comprehensive guides and troubleshooting resources
- **Best Practices**: Follow blockchain network guidelines and recommendations

### Community Engagement
- **Respectful Communication**: Professional and courteous interactions
- **Knowledge Sharing**: Contribute to blockchain ecosystem education
- **Collaboration**: Work constructively with other validators and service providers
- **Support**: Assist community members with technical questions and guidance

---

## Operational Ethics

### Validator Operations
- **No Double Signing**: Strict prevention of consensus violations
- **Honest Participation**: Faithful execution of blockchain protocols
- **Network Health**: Prioritize network stability and decentralization
- **Fair Competition**: Compete ethically with other validators

### Service Provider Standards
- **Fair Pricing**: Competitive and transparent fee structures
- **No Discrimination**: Equal service access regardless of stake size
- **Privacy Protection**: Safeguard user data and transaction privacy
- **Honest Marketing**: Accurate representation of services and capabilities

### Financial Responsibility
- **Transparent Fees**: Clear disclosure of all service charges
- **No Hidden Costs**: Upfront communication of all expenses
- **Reward Distribution**: Timely and accurate reward processing
- **Financial Security**: Proper custody and protection of delegated assets

---

## Community Standards

### Acceptable Behavior
- Professional communication in all channels
- Constructive feedback and technical discussions
- Respect for diverse perspectives and approaches
- Collaborative problem-solving and knowledge sharing
- Adherence to blockchain network governance processes

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language
- Spreading misinformation about blockchain networks or competitors
- Attempting to manipulate network consensus or governance
- Violating user privacy or security
- Engaging in fraudulent or deceptive practices

## Enforcement and Accountability

### Self-Monitoring
- Regular performance audits and improvement plans
- Proactive communication about service issues or changes
- Continuous education on blockchain best practices
- Investment in infrastructure improvements and security updates

### Community Accountability
- Open communication channels for feedback and concerns
